Creamy Fettuccine

Back to Home

Description

Ingredients

  • 375g fettuccine pasta
  • 175g shortcut bacon rashers, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 20g butter
  • 2 tsp fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup cooking cream
  • 1/3 cup parmesan, finely grated

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. While pasta is cooking, melt butter in a fry pan over medium heat. Add bacon and cook for 4 minutes or until golden.
  3. Add garlic and rosemary, and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Whisk eggs, yolks, cream and parmesan in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Drain pasta and return to saucepan off the heat.
  6. Add bacon mixture and egg mixture to pasta. Toss to combine.
